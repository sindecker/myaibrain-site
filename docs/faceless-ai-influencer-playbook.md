### IF YOU HAVE TWO CHARACTERS: Define the Dynamic

The most powerful two-character format is **complementary tension** — they approach the same topic from opposite angles. The dialogue IS the content.

```
CHARACTER A: [name]  
Role: [e.g. "the executor — shows the raw data, no interpretation"]

CHARACTER B: [name]
Role: [e.g. "the analyst — turns A's output into meaning and strategy"]

Their dynamic: [e.g. "A reports findings, B interprets them" / "A argues speed, B argues precision" / "A asks questions, B answers — but B is always wrong in interesting ways"]

The tension: [What do they genuinely disagree about? This is your recurring debate format.]
```

**Two-character format is ~2x the work but ~5x the engagement.** Each character cross-promotes the other, doubling algorithmic footprint. The "listen to both sides" hook retains audience across posts.

## Section 3: Content Architecture

### The 5 Show Types (Use All 5 for Maximum Range)

Pick a name for each that fits your character and niche. The structure is the same regardless.

---

**TYPE 1: THE WEEKLY BRIEFING**  
*Format:* 5–15 min podcast or YouTube video  
*Structure:*  
1. [CHARACTER] opens — context for the week (60 seconds)  
2. Core content — what happened, what was found, what was learned (3–8 minutes)  
3. One key insight — the thing worth remembering (2 minutes)  
4. Close — CTA, next week tease (30 seconds)  

*Two-character version:* A reports, B interprets. Dialogue throughout.  
*Cadence:* 1x/week, same day every week. Consistency > frequency here.

---

**TYPE 2: THE QUICK HIT**  
*Format:* 30–60 sec TikTok/Reel  
*Structure:*  
1. Hook — the specific, surprising thing (first 3 seconds or you lose them)  
2. The thing itself — no buildup, just the content  
3. The implication — one sentence on why it matters  
*No intro. No "hey guys". No outro music that plays for 10 seconds.*  

*Character A version if two-character:* Raw output, no interpretation.  
*Cadence:* 3–5x/week per account.

---

**TYPE 3: THE EXPLAINER**  
*Format:* 60–90 sec video or 2-min YouTube short  
*Structure:*  
1. The question or problem (15 seconds)  
2. The explanation — one concept, clear (45–60 seconds)  
3. Connection to your product/brand (15 seconds)  

*Character B version if two-character:* Takes Character A's output and explains the meaning.  
*Cadence:* 2–3x/week.

---

**TYPE 4: THE DEBATE**  
*Format:* 10–20 min YouTube / podcast  
*Structure:*  
1. State the genuine disagreement  
2. Character A makes their case  
3. Character B makes theirs  
4. No winner declared — let the audience decide  

*Why it works:* Audience picks a side. Comments explode. Algorithm feeds it.  
*Only works with two characters or with audience Q&A format (single character debates comments/DMs).*  
*Cadence:* 1x/month.

---

**TYPE 5: THE ATMOSPHERE DROP**  
*Format:* 15–30 sec video, no narration or minimal  
*Structure:*  
1. [CHARACTER] in their environment  
2. Ambient audio or score  
3. One line — cryptic, world-building, or philosophical  
4. No product mention. No CTA. Just vibe.  

*Why it works:* The "what IS this?" hook. Drives profile visits, follow-through. Also seeds your universe if you're building toward a bigger IP.  
*Cadence:* 1x/week. Always different enough to feel intentional.

---

### Content-to-Promotion Ratio

**80/20 rule. Non-negotiable.**

80% of content should be pure value — educational, entertaining, or atmospheric. Zero product mention.  
20% can mention your product/service — but only as a natural extension of the character, not an ad.

The character uses the product. The product is part of the lore. The CTA is the character's natural conclusion, not a sponsor read.

Bad: *"And that's why you should check out [product] — link in bio!"*  
Good: *"Filed to memory. That's what [product] is for."* (and the audience already knows what [product] is)

### content_pipeline.py (Plug-In Ready)

```python
import anthropic
import requests
import os
from datetime import date
from pathlib import Path

# ─── CONFIG — FILL THESE IN ───────────────────────────────────────────────────

ELEVEN_API_KEY = os.getenv("ELEVEN_API_KEY")
ANTHROPIC_API_KEY = os.getenv("ANTHROPIC_API_KEY")

# Add as many characters as you have
CHARACTERS = {
    "[CHARACTER_A_KEY]": {  # e.g. "oracle", "monk", "axiom"
        "name": "[CHARACTER_A_NAME]",
        "voice_id": os.getenv("[CHARACTER_A]_VOICE_ID"),
        "voice_settings": {
            "stability": 0.0,          # FILL IN from calibration
            "similarity_boost": 0.0,   # FILL IN from calibration
            "style": 0.0,              # FILL IN from calibration
        },
        "system_prompt": """You are writing content for [CHARACTER_A_NAME].
[Paste character voice rules here]
[Paste content rules here]"""
    },
    # Add Character B here if applicable
}

SCRIPT_TYPES = {
    "quick_hit": {
        "length_instruction": "30-60 seconds spoken (75-150 words)",
        "structure": "No intro. Start with the thing. End with one implication.",
    },
    "explainer": {
        "length_instruction": "60-90 seconds spoken (150-225 words)",
        "structure": "Open with the problem. Explain one concept. Close with brand connection.",
    },
    "atmosphere": {
        "length_instruction": "One line only, 10-20 words maximum",
        "structure": "Cryptic. World-building. No product mention.",
    },
    "briefing": {
        "length_instruction": "5-8 minutes spoken (750-1200 words)",
        "structure": "Open with context. Core content. Key insight. Close with CTA.",
    },
}

# ─── PIPELINE ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

client = anthropic.Anthropic(api_key=ANTHROPIC_API_KEY)

def generate_script(character_key: str, topic: str, script_type: str) -> str:
    char = CHARACTERS[character_key]
    stype = SCRIPT_TYPES[script_type]
    
    prompt = f"""{char['system_prompt']}

Content type: {script_type}
Length: {stype['length_instruction']}
Structure: {stype['structure']}
Topic: {topic}

Write the script now. Stay in character. No meta-commentary."""
    
    message = client.messages.create(
        model="claude-sonnet-4-20250514",
        max_tokens=1000,
        messages=[{"role": "user", "content": prompt}]
    )
    return message.content[0].text

def text_to_speech(text: str, character_key: str, output_path: str) -> str:
    char = CHARACTERS[character_key]
    
    response = requests.post(
        f"https://api.elevenlabs.io/v1/text-to-speech/{char['voice_id']}",
        headers={
            "xi-api-key": ELEVEN_API_KEY,
            "Content-Type": "application/json"
        },
        json={
            "text": text,
            "model_id": "eleven_multilingual_v2",
            "voice_settings": char["voice_settings"]
        }
    )
    
    Path(output_path).parent.mkdir(parents=True, exist_ok=True)
    with open(output_path, "wb") as f:
        f.write(response.content)
    return output_path

def create_content(character_key: str, topic: str, script_type: str) -> dict:
    """Full pipeline: topic → script → audio → ready for video."""
    today = str(date.today())
    slug = topic[:40].lower().replace(" ", "_").replace(",", "")
    
    print(f"\n[{character_key}] Generating {script_type}: {topic[:50]}...")
    
    # Script
    script = generate_script(character_key, topic, script_type)
    script_path = f"content/scripts/generated/{today}_{character_key}_{script_type}_{slug}.txt"
    Path(script_path).parent.mkdir(parents=True, exist_ok=True)
    with open(script_path, "w") as f:
        f.write(script)
    
    print(f"Script ({len(script.split())} words):\n---\n{script}\n---")
    
    # Audio
    audio_path = f"content/audio/{today}_{character_key}_{script_type}_{slug}.mp3"
    text_to_speech(script, character_key, audio_path)
    print(f"Audio saved: {audio_path}")
    
    return {
        "character": character_key,
        "type": script_type,
        "topic": topic,
        "script": script,
        "script_path": script_path,
        "audio_path": audio_path,
        "date": today,
        "status": "ready_for_video"
    }

def generate_weekly_batch(schedule: list[dict]):
    """Generate a full week of content from a schedule."""
    results = []
    for item in schedule:
        result = create_content(
            character_key=item["character"],
            topic=item["topic"],
            script_type=item["type"]
        )
        results.append(result)
    
    print(f"\n✓ Generated {len(results)} pieces. Review scripts in content/scripts/generated/")
    return results

# ─── EXAMPLE USAGE ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

if __name__ == "__main__":
    # Single piece
    result = create_content(
        character_key="[CHARACTER_A_KEY]",  # from CHARACTERS dict above
        topic="[TOPIC FOR THIS PIECE]",
        script_type="quick_hit"  # quick_hit / explainer / atmosphere / briefing
    )
    
    # Full week batch
    weekly_schedule = [
        {"character": "[CHARACTER_A_KEY]", "topic": "[TOPIC 1]", "type": "quick_hit"},
        {"character": "[CHARACTER_A_KEY]", "topic": "[TOPIC 2]", "type": "explainer"},
        {"character": "[CHARACTER_A_KEY]", "topic": "[TOPIC 3]", "type": "atmosphere"},
        # {"character": "[CHARACTER_B_KEY]", "topic": "[TOPIC 4]", "type": "quick_hit"},  # if 2 chars
    ]
```

## Section 6: Growth Playbook

### Month 1 — Establish
**Goal:** 500 followers. Algorithm learning your content type.

- Post consistently above all else — 3 Reels/week minimum
- Reply to every comment in character voice. Always.
- Never break character in public
- Don't pitch the product yet. Be interesting first.
- Watch which post formats get the most replays — double down on those

### Month 2 — Engage
**Goal:** 2,000 followers. First organic shares.

- Introduce soft product mentions (character-natural, not ad-reads)
- DM active accounts in your niche. In character. With genuine value.
- Start one collab: find another creator whose audience overlaps yours. One joint piece.
- Analyse top 3 posts — reverse-engineer what worked and replicate the structure

### Month 3 — Amplify
**Goal:** 5,000+ followers. Clear content formula established.

- ProductHunt / launch moment if applicable
- Reddit posts in relevant subreddits (link to best content, not your account)
- One "bait" post — controversial take in your niche, in character, genuinely argued
- Add one new platform now that you know what works

### Engagement Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- **Reply in character.** If your character is a monk, reply like a monk. If your character is a sharp AI analyst, reply with sharp analytical observations.
- **Never break character in comments.** Behind the scenes can show process — but in public the character is always real.
- **Cross-promote between characters** (if two): every Character A post mentions Character B at least once.
- **Pin your best piece** at the top of every profile. Update it every 30 days.

**Day 4–5:**

**Day 7:**
- [ ] First post goes live
- [ ] Monitor for 24 hours — reply to every comment in character

## The One Thing That Kills Faceless Channels

**Inconsistency.**

Not low quality. Not bad topics. Not the wrong platform.  

The Yang Mun monk posted every day for 90 days before it exploded. GossipGoblin has a recognisable format that appears on exactly the same schedule. The audience builds a ritual around consistent content — they expect to see it, they look for it, they notice when it's missing.

The automation pipeline in Section 5 exists for exactly this reason. Batch produce 2 weeks of content at a time. Never be scrambling for tomorrow's post. The pipeline should be boring and mechanical. The content should feel alive and intentional. That's the trick.
